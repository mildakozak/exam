Frontløberne is an open platform for cultural and creative entrepreneurship. We believe in your ideas and we are happy to share our experience with you!

We came a long way to be here

with you today.

A large group of young people has been organizing cultural activities on the streets of Aarhus over a long period of time. In 1989, they have officially founded Frontløberne - a voice of youth culture where everyone was welcome to express their creative side.

Through out the years

so many projects have been embarked.

After so much time our projects have been taking many directions.

But one thing has stayed the same.

The youth has been always setting the agenda.

We have supported and guided so many creative souls during our incredible journey.

Now we need your support.

We are at risk of losing our beloved house where Frontløberne has been growing and expanding throughout its years of activity.

The memories and experiences shared in that place are so precious to us. We have built our own creative world there and we can’t imagine losing it.

Luckily, it is also not in our nature to give up. Sign the petition and help us continue breaking the norms of what’s possible!
