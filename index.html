WHO ARE WE?

Frontløberne at its core is a creative community. We launch, develop and execute cultural productions of all kinds. We work with a wide range of diverse young-minded entrepreneurs and creators. Our goal is to keep on creating new and exciting cultural and social values and continue to break the norms of what is possible!

BECOME A MEMBER

Everybody is welcome to take part in our community and to participate in our projects. But if you are interested in having your own working space, consider becoming a member. We provide tools, access to our network, inspiration and professional guidance for people with new ideas and initiatives of cultural projects and events. If you or your organisation needs more space, don't hesitate to contact us.
